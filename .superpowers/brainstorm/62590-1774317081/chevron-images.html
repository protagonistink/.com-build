On Cropping — CDN vs Canva
✓ Good news: Sanity's CDN handles crops via URL params (?w=W&h=H&fit=crop&crop=top|center|bottom). No Canva needed for the full-scroll screenshots — we can programmatically crop to any window. However, if any screenshot has a specific section that reads better than the auto-crop, Canva would let you export a precise frame.

The CDN crop approach is faster to implement and editable in code. Canva gives pixel-perfect control if a specific moment in the scroll tells the story better than top/center/bottom.
