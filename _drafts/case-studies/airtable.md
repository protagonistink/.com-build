## SOURCE CONTENT (from pkthewriter)

**Context (headline):**
After years helping startups and agencies kick some ass, we helped the cloud-based collaboration SaaS launch their first national campaign.

**The Conflict:**
The line between "wow, this is cool" and "what does this even do?" is razor-thin. Product specs weren't going to work. Some people may call it a souped-up Excel, but those who use Airtable know it's far more powerful. We needed to show what software could do.

**The Resolution:**
We created four :15 spots, each built around real, high-stakes moments where teams need to move together. Instead of specs or demos, the work focused on rhythm, precision, and language — showing how work flows when the system actually works.

Every word had to land at exactly the right moment. The pacing did the explaining. The story carried the meaning. We let the moments shine, kept details implicit, and trusted viewers to understand exactly how they could use the product.

**Disciplines:** Creative Direction · Copywriting · Scriptwriting
**Deliverables:** Four :15 Spots · Radio/Podcast Scripts · Social Strategy & Copy

**Impact:**
- We established Airtable's voice at a national level.
- We broke through the SaaS market by focusing on purpose vs. demo.
- We didn't teach the product, we showed the results.
